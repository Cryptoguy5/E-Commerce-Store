Our design philosophy doesn’t have much to do with design at all

We started our company because it felt like no one in the furniture industry was listening. Legacy retailers churned out one trendy, flimsy product after another, and the second they had your cash, you were on your own. But like we said, we’re not a normal furniture company. We’re committed to designing a different experience, something completely new that’s not just created for you, but with you.

We’re solving the biggest problems in furniture

Free shipping

Since our furniture can fit in boxes that normal delivery networks can accommodate, we’re able to offer free delivery on every single order — which can save folks over $100 on big items like a couch.

Simple assembly

This isn’t your parents’ flat-pack furniture. It’s the next generation of modular, designed for easy, tool-free assembly that can be done in minutes.

Made to expand

Whether it’s adding a seat to a loveseat to make a sofa, or adding shelving units over time, our furniture is designed to grow with you..

We are more than just a furniture company!

Selling furniture is just a small part of what we do. We aim to design new styles, advance what furniture can do for our customers, develop and give back to the community, and help heal the environment.

Beautiful Designs that Last a Long Time

Direct relationship

By doing everything ourselves — and selling directly to people — we can run a more nimble & efficient business. But it’s not just about savings: it also allows us to have a close feedback loop with customers and iterate on our designs way faster than the competition.

Curated catalog

We strip away a lot of needless decisions and let people create their perfect piece without an overwhelming sea of choices.

Support and service

Our team of experts is always standing by to go above and beyond to lend a hand, take feedback, or mail out a replacement part.
