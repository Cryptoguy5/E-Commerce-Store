Returns and Warranties

Policy basics

Returns

We’re here to create a better furniture shopping experience from top to bottom, and that includes a 30-day return policy for any Furniture Co products. Additionally, we offer a 100-day return policy on our Lyric Mattresses.

In order to offer fast and free shipping, reduce waste, and responsibly recycle or refurbish returned items, we charge a return shipping fee equal to 10% of what you paid for the product, with the original packaging intact. If you don't have the original packaging, the fee is 20% of the paid value. The fee is always capped at $250, per returned item. Note: Mattress returns are free only if the mattress is in good condition; if the mattress is soiled or otherwise unsalable there is a $100 pick-up fee.

Of course, defective or damaged orders are covered by our warranty and aren't subject to any return shipping fees. For those cases, check out our return eligibility information below.

Refunds will be issued to the original form of payment within 48 hours of confirmation that the shipping carrier is in possession of your return. Depending on your financial institution it may take up to 2 weeks to receive your refund.

How to initiate a return

Please begin by completing the Return Form.

Once completed, our Customer Experience team will contact you directly within 24-48 hours with next steps. Furniture Co will either send a return shipping label or schedule a return pickup. Furniture Co reserves the right to choose the method of return.

The return process must be initiated within 30 calendar days of receipt for all products, with the sole exception of mattresses. The return process for mattresses must be initiated within 100 days of receipt. Receipt is when the last package for an individual product is delivered per shipping carrier confirmation and not when the order is unboxed.

Note: Photos clearly depicting the issue are required for any damaged or defective product returns or replacement requests. Without photos, Furniture Co reserves the right to assess a return shipping fee.

Return Eligibility

Products must be in usable condition (e.g., no stains, tears, burns, pet damage, odors or other soiling, etc.) to be eligible for return. Usable condition is determined solely at Furniture Co's judgment. Returns are accepted or rejected at Furniture Co's sole discretion. Furniture Co may request descriptions and/or images of the Products to determine the state of said Products prior to accepting returns. Failure to comply with Furniture Co's requests for any reason may result in Furniture Co's refusal of return.

  • Products shipped outside of the continental United States are not eligible for return.
  • Missing an appointment for a return to be picked up from your address will incur a fee. This fee will be clearly disclosed at the time Furniture Co schedules a pickup on the customer's behalf.
  • Individually purchased components or add-ons that were not part of your original order are not eligible for returns.
  • Slight color variation (to be solely determined by Furniture Co) and level of firmness are considered a matter of preference and not a defect. Products may be returned for these reasons within the return window, but will incur return shipping fees as described above.
