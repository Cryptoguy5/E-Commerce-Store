Free shipping on every single order

No minimums, no fine print, just a simple promise to get your new furniture home as soon as we can, for free. Other furniture companies charge outrageous ‘special freight’ fees to put one couch on one truck, which is a costly, and not to mention unsustainable, way of doing things. But thanks to our modular design, we pack things in manageable boxes and ship them to you just like a normal package.

A quick word on delivery timelines

We’ve always prided ourselves on some of the fastest delivery timelines in the business. Before the pandemic, we delivered orders within a week, and we intend to get back to that standard. Until then, we encourage you to check the shipping estimates, which can be found below or on the product page for any given item, right under the “Add to Cart” button. You can also take a look at our Ready to Ship section, which has everything that’s in stock and will arrive within four weeks.
